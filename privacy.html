Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

This website ("Still No Education") is a personal campaign website created by the parents of a child currently without suitable education provision.

We are committed to respecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data through this website.

  • There are no user accounts.
  • There are no contact forms.
  • We do not use analytics tools.
  • We do not use advertising trackers.

If you contact us directly via email, we will receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.

3. Email Contact

If you email us:

  • Your message will be stored in our email account.
  • We will only use your information to respond to your enquiry.
  • We will not share your email address with third parties unless required by law.

4. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or analytics purposes.

However, basic technical cookies may be used automatically by the hosting provider (e.g., GitHub Pages) for security and functionality purposes. These are outside of our control.

5. Hosting

This website is hosted via GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical log information such as IP addresses for security and operational purposes. Please refer to GitHub's privacy policy for further details.

6. Data Security

We do not actively collect or store user data via this website.

Any correspondence sent to us via email is handled privately and stored securely within our email provider.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact:
